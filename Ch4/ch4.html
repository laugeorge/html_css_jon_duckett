Arc Shot

A shot in which the subject is photgraphed by an encircling or moving camera





Interlude

A brief, intervening film scene or sequence, not specifically tied to the plot





Prologue

A speech, preface, introduction, or brief scene preceding the main action or plot of a film: contrast to epilogue
